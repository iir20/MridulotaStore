About MRIDULOTA

Born from ancient Bengali traditions and a passion for natural skincare, MRIDULOTA creates handmade soaps using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Each soap is carefully crafted with 100% natural ingredients, free from harsh chemicals and artificial fragrances. We believe in the power of nature to nourish and heal your skin, while supporting sustainable and eco-friendly practices.
